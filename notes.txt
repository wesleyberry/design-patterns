A design patterns describes how you should coordinate your classes

Design patterns fall into 3 categories:
    Creational
    Structural
    Behavioral  

Coupling describes how much a class is dependent upon another class
    car and tire analogy

Interfaces - a contract that specifies the capabilities that a class should provide
    Restaurant and chef analogy

Encapsulation - used to hide values or state inside of a class

Abstraction - reduce complexity by hiding unnecessary details
    DVR analogy
    Remote control analogy

Inheritance - inheriting properties from other classes
